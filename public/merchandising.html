A live Shopify-fed range board ranked by recent sales, stock depth, and gross margin when product costs are available. Use it for buying reviews, trade meetings, and print-ready merchandising plans.

Shopify connection
Set SHOPIFY_SHOP, SHOPIFY_CLIENT_ID, and SHOPIFY_CLIENT_SECRET on the server. Optional: SHOPIFY_API_VERSION. The page exchanges those credentials server-side, so secrets are never exposed in browser code.

Merchandising key

Modes

Commercial

Ranks the strongest overall products for a general trade view.

50% sales + 25% stock + 25% GP
Reorder

Prioritises strong sellers that are running low or have gone oversold.

50% sales + 35% low stock + 15% GP
Markdown

Finds high-stock products with weaker sales and weaker margin.

45% stock + 35% low sales + 20% low GP
Feature

Finds products worth pushing because sales, margin, and depth can support demand.

55% sales + 25% GP + 20% stock
Gold Dust

Finds products with low GA4 views but strong Shopify units per view.

55% CVR + 35% low views + 10% GP
Needs Work

Finds products getting attention but not converting into unit sales.

55% views + 35% low CVR + 10% stock

Action Badges

  • Feature: strong sales with enough stock behind it.
  • Reorder: strong sales with low stock.
  • Markdown: low sales with high stock.
  • Gold Dust: low views with high CVR. Candidate for more exposure.
  • Needs Work: high views with low CVR. Candidate for content, price, stock, or product-page review.
  • Sold out: sold units with zero or negative available stock.
  • Watch: does not strongly fit another action yet.

Core Metrics

  • Sales: order line revenue in the selected window.
  • Units: order line quantity in the selected window.
  • Stock: available Shopify inventory across variants. Negative stock is shown because it can signal oversold items.
  • Sell-through: units sold divided by units sold plus current stock.
  • GP: calculated from Shopify price and variant cost per item. If cost is missing, the card shows No cost.
  • Views: GA4 item views matched by item ID, SKU, Shopify IDs, handle, or item name.
  • ATC: GA4 items added to cart.
  • CVR: Shopify units sold in the selected period divided by GA4 item views for the same period.

Score Logic

Scores compare each product against the current filtered view. Sales strength is revenue divided by the highest revenue in view. Stock strength is available stock divided by the highest stock in view. GP strength is GP percentage divided by 100.

sell-through = units / (units + max(stock, 0))

If no GP data is available, Commercial mode falls back to 67% sales and 33% stock. If some products are missing GP, they use the average GP of costed products for scoring so missing cost does not automatically sink them. GA4 metrics are displayed for context, but they do not currently change the Merch score.
